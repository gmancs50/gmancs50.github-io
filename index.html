Simple Hamburger

Easy Burgers Anytime

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 10 mins
Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional seasonings (e.g., garlic powder, onion powder, paprika)
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional, for cheeseburgers)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Pickles, sliced
  • Ketchup, mustard, or other desired condiments
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for grilling or pan-frying)

Method

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine ground beef with salt, pepper, and any desired seasonings. Mix gently until just combined.
  3. Form the mixture into patties, making a slight indentation in the center of each patty.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or in the pan. Cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C) for medium doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese to each patty, if desired, and close the grill lid or cover the pan to melt the cheese.
  6. While the patties are cooking, toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster until golden brown.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom buns. Top with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and your favorite condiments.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade hamburgers!
